Their Story

Alex and Taylor met during a wildlife conservation project in the forests of Oregon. Their first interaction was anything but ordinary - both were trying to photograph the same elusive red squirrel when they literally bumped into each other.

The "staring contest" story refers to their third date, when they went hiking and encountered a particularly bold squirrel. Taylor insisted they could outstare the creature, while Alex laughed and took pictures. After what felt like an eternity, the squirrel eventually scurried away, but Taylor claimed victory anyway.

This became their metaphor for relationships - sometimes you think you're winning by being stubborn, but real connection requires knowing when to look away and laugh together.

They now run a small wildlife sanctuary together and continue to find joy in the simple, silly moments that brought them together.
