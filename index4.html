My Story

I'm a passionate wildlife photographer based in the beautiful Pacific Northwest. Growing up surrounded by nature, I developed a deep appreciation for the environment and all its creatures.

My journey into photography began during college when I volunteered for a wildlife conservation project. I discovered that through my lens, I could capture the beauty of nature and share it with others, inspiring them to protect our planet.

When I'm not out in the wilderness with my camera, you can find me exploring local coffee shops, reading in a quiet corner of a bookstore, or practicing yoga at sunrise. I believe in living intentionally and finding joy in life's simple pleasures.

I'm looking for someone who shares my love for adventure and appreciation for quiet moments. Someone who understands that the best conversations happen during long walks and that sometimes the most memorable dates are the simplest ones.
